Hi, I'm Mumuxu!

I just recently graduated from McMaster University with a bachelors of electrical engineering. After many enduring years at school I have learned an array of skills and developed a passion for code that I believe has set me up for future successes in the industry. Being technically inclined since my childhood I have picked up various competencies along the way, be it in the classroom or through extracurriculars like FIRST robotics or Formula Electric.

Beyond the robots and computers however, some of the best words of advice given to me was that "University is a place of metamorphosis for a student/person". During the years as an undergraduate I not only doubled down on my passions for technology, but I also found a new love for meditation, spirituality, literature, music and so much more. No doubt this experience has transformed me as an individual and as one phase of my life comes to a close I am eager to bring myself and my experiences with me to the workplace.
